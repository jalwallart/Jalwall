Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our custom water bubble wall solutions.

What is a water bubble wall?

A water bubble wall is a modern decorative feature where bubbles flow through clear panels of water.

LED lighting enhances the bubbles, creating a calming and eye-catching visual effect that works beautifully in both homes and commercial spaces.

Where can bubble walls be installed?

Bubble walls can be installed in many interior spaces where you want to create a premium and relaxing atmosphere.

Popular locations include living rooms, entryways, offices, hotels, showrooms, restaurants, and reception areas.

Are bubble walls suitable for Indian homes and businesses?

Yes. Our bubble wall designs are created specifically for Indian interiors and commercial environments.

They work well in modern homes as well as offices, hotels, showrooms, and other business spaces across India.

Can I customise the size and design?

Absolutely. Every water bubble wall we create is fully custom designed.

We tailor the size, panel design, and LED lighting to match your interior space, style preferences, and installation requirements.
